Information Architecture

Get Started Entry Points

For quarterly software releases at Intel, I worked with our User Expereince team to optimize the experience for developers who were new to the Intel® oneAPI ecosystem of compilers, libraries, and tools. We determined where users were coming from and where to guide them for the next step.

Get Started Next Steps

After a user completes the get started steps, they run a sample to ensure the system is working correctly. From there, the user gets directed to next level steps or the troubleshooting guide.
